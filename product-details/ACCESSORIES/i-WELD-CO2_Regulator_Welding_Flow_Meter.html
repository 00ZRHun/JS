i-WELD CO2 Regulator Welding Flow Meter

Made of high quality PC and copper, sturdy and durable in use.

Use economic style flow meter regulator and single stage piston design, combination of regulator and flow meter as one unit.

The output flow is adjustable from 0 to 25 lpm, and is indicated by a ball which moves up and down in the flow tube, so this flow meter will handle MIG and TIG welding both steel and aluminum.

Easy to operate, convenient for use.
