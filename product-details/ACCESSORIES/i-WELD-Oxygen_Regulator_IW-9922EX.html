i-WELD Oxygen Regulator IW-9922EX

The i-WELD IW-9922 Oxygen Regulator is a single-stage, solid brass regulator designed for medium duty welding, cutting and brazing applications. The i-WELD IW-9922 oxygen regulator comes with dual 2-inch steel pressure gauges and has a max outlet pressure rating of 145 PSI. Ergonomic adjusting knob provides precise operation and longer service life.
