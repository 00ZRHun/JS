i-WELD Acetylene Regulator IW-9922EY

The i-WELD IW-9922 Acetylene Regulator is a single-stage, solid brass regulator designed for medium duty welding, cutting and brazing applications. The i-WELD acetylene regulator comes with dual 2-inch steel pressure gauges and has a max outlet pressure rating of 20 PSI. Ergonomic adjusting knob provides precise operation and longer service life.

The i-WELD IW-9922 acetylene regulator comes with an inlet (left hand, male threads) and is designed to be used on acetylene cylinders equipped with a valve (left hand, female threads).
