i-WELD WELDING HELMET S998 Graffiti

Auto-darkening helmets are designed with the serious user in mind and conform with ANSI and CE standards. The nylon helmet features a moisture absorbing headband which adjusts up and down as well as front to back. The ratcheting headgear allows for many hours of comfortable use. Select welding or grinding state on outside of helmet; shade number on the outside.

FEATURES:

       ● Adjust sensitivity outside helmet
       ● Welding or grinding selection
       ● UV/IR protection
       ● Battery and Solar Power
       ● Dark to clear adjustment
       ● Moulded nylon construction
       ● Variable shades 4-8/9-13
       ● Replaceable clear cover lens

Specifications:

Viewing Area (mm) : 95 x 36
Sensors : Two infrared detector
UVIR Protection : Up to Shade DIN16
Light State : Shade DIN 4
Variable Shade : From DIN9 – DIN13
Power Supply : Solar cells + Lithium battery
Sensitivity : Adjustable
Reaction Time : 0.00005 sec (1/20,000s)
Delay Time : Can vary at 0.3s – 0.9s
Dark to Light : 0.25 ~ 0.8 s
Operating Temperature : -5°C ~ +55°C
Storing Temperature   : -20°C ~ +70°C
Helmet Material : Polyamide (Nylon)
Weight (g) : 470

Package Includes:

       1 x Welding Helmet
       1 x English manual
       1 x Hood
